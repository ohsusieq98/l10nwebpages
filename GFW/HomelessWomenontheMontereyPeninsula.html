Homeless Women on the Monterey Peninsula

The goal of this assessment is to document the primary causes of homelessness for women, needs and challenges, existing services, local policy considerations, and other recommendations. This assessment was commissioned by the Community Foundation for Monterey County (CFMC) on behalf of the Fund for Homeless Women, a eld of interest fund of the CFMC. Data was gathered through primary and secondary sources. Primary source data included contributions from 38 service providers, public officials and the 60 homeless women who generously participated in surveys, interviews and group conversations. A policy scan was also conducted to identify policies and environmental factors that impact homeless women on the Monterey Peninsula.
